Instructions:

  1. Press the space key to start a new game
  2. When you're ready, click inside the game to play!

How to Play:

  • An item of trash will appear on the screen
  • While the item mover around,
    try to decide whether the item should be
    composted, recycled, or trashed,
    When the item stops in the red box,
    Use your arrow keys to sort it.
    (left for compost, right for trash, down for recycle)
  • If you sort it in the wrong spot,
    you will lose a life. You have 10 lives!

  • Try to get a better score than your friends!
